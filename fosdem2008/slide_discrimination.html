Web Discrimination

  • Days of "only works with MSIE 4+ or Netscape 4+" are mostly over
  • Nowadays it's "only works with MSIE 6+ or Firefox 1.5+"
  • Most such sites work fine with Gecko itself, but not with non-FF UAs
  • This is not about some obscure tech religious stuff
  • Discriminating against clients, limiting choice on the browser market
  • We'd like sites to Just Work with all standards based browsers
  • Need to work with mozilla.org on educating web developers
  • Never completely shut out unknown browsers
  • Maybe display warning page and let users continue
  • Idea: extension for FF, etc. that warns people about discriminating sites
    • Maybe doing transparent but automatic UA-spoofing as needed
    • Hooks for "tech evang" / anti-discrimination activites
    • Would perfectly follow the Mozilla Manifesto - maybe something for Mozilla Labs?
