Driving a Volunteer Project

  • Volunteers work on what they like ("scratching their itches")
  • Can only do things as they have time
  • Project management can't force developer priorities
  • Constant poking of people to get things done
  • No firm authority for project management/coordination
  • Volunteers who can do the really hard things are rare (see nsIClonableDeveloper!)
