Community Marketing

  • No marketing budget
  • Word of mouth is probably the major channel
  • Website needs to make a good impression (seen seamonkey-project.org yet?)
  • T-shirts, etc. are nice, there are decent web shops, but intl shipping and customs are expensive
  • Logos and buttons for spreading the word on websites
  • Press reviews - small projects are lucky if some reporters like them
  • Don't underestimate the blogosphere when targeting advanced web users
  • Try to reach your primary target audience(s)
  • As a browser, you easily lose users when websites don't work
