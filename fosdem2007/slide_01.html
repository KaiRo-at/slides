Key Facts

  • SeaMonkey internet application suite is successor of Mozilla suite, based on open-sourced "Netscape Communicator" code.
  • Community project took over in March 2005
  • SeaMonkey 1.0 released in January 2006
  • SeaMonkey 1.1 released in January 2007
  • 1.0.x and 1.1.x security/stability releases in sync with Gecko/Firefox security updates
  • Latest release: SeaMonkey 1.1.1 (and 1.0.8), February 2007 (actually both upcoming next week)
